Noisy Sinusoid

In reality, sensors are cheap, the cheaper the sensor is, the noisier the signal is. Here, we 'nosified' our perfect sinusoid.


Moving Average

There are multiple ways to clean up a signal, the simplest way is taking moving average.


Gaussian Filter

A less naive method is taking a weighted average, for example, the Gaussian Filter.

Gaussian First Order

Whats interesting about the Gussian Filter is that you can take derivative on the filter itself, and the weighted average calculated will also be taken derivative. This is very useful when you are detecting for peaks, simply put on a First Order Gaussian filter and find the zeroes in the filtered signal.
